Scottish Terrier

Populary known as the Scottie

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height: 25 cm or 9.8 in
  • Average Lifespan: 11 to 13 years
  • Habitat: Prairies, grasslands, forests, rain forests, deserts, coastal regions and arctic zones.

The Scottish Terrier is also known as the Arberdeen Terrier. As a terrier is one of the five breeds that originated in Scotland.
